# Future Expansion: Race Modifiers Vault
*This file contains mechanical ideas, rule variations, and card traits reserved for future development phases. These elements are explicitly excluded from the current Proof of Concept (PoC) prototype.*

---

### Energy Channeling
- **Description:** Dead cards drawn for card suits that have already crossed the finish line and concluded their race are not wasted. Instead, their values can be captured and channeled by your active horse to trigger alternate actions or secondary boosts.
- **Beneficiary:** Self
- **Upgrades:** 
  - **Overcharge:** Channeling cards of an exact matching dead suit grants an additional +1 speed modifier.

### Suit Synchronization
- **Description:** Forces a drastic state shift by turning all track-cards layout into a single, uniform suit—including your own active suit. This radically simplifies movement mechanics for a duration or across the setup layout.
- **Beneficiary:** Global (State Change)
- **Upgrades:** 
  - **Selective Sifting:** Upgrading this modifier protects your personal active suit from being converted, allowing you to maintain an asymmetrical tactical advantage over opponents.

### Inverted Polarity
- **Description:** Track cards that would normally push the matching horse one step backward instead push it one step forward. The fundamental direction of track-card effects is reversed for all players.
- **Beneficiary:** Global (State Change)
- **Upgrades:**
  - **Selective Reversal:** The polarity inversion only applies to cards matching your active suit — other suits resolve normally (backward) as they would on a standard track.
  - **Chaos Circuit:** Each track card now has a 50% chance (coin-flip) to either push forward or push backward, applied independently per trigger. Future upgrades can shift the odds in your favor.

### Reverse Joker
- **Description:** When a Joker card is drawn, instead of all non-leading horses advancing one step (the base Joker stumble mechanic), the leading horse(s) regress one step instead. The Joker no longer helps the pack catch up — it solely punishes whoever is in front. A horse cannot regress below step 0.
- **Beneficiary:** Global (State Change)
- **Upgrades:**
  - **Mass Momentum Collapse:** If more than one horse is tied for the lead, each tied horse regresses N steps (where N = the number of tied horses), instead of the usual 1. A horse cannot regress below step 0. At 4 tied horses, each would regress 4 steps.
